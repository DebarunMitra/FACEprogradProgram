Color Table With Philosophical Value

# Color Philosophical Value Industries Use
1 RED Red is the color of energy, passion, action, ambition and determination. Entertainment, Food, Sport, Fire Protection, Children Product
2 ORANGE Orange is the color of social communication and optimism. From a negative color meaning it is also a sign of pessimism and superficiality. Art, Food, Sport, Transportation
3 YELLOW yellow is the color of the mind and the intellect. It is optimistic and cheerful. However it can also suggest impatience, criticism and cowardice. Food, Sport, Transportation, Travel, Leisure
4 GREEN Green is the color of balance and growth. It can mean both self-reliance as a positive and possessiveness as a negative, among many other meanings. Environment, Banking, Real Estate Farming, Non Profit
5 PINK The color psychology of pink is unconditional love and nurturing. Pink can also be immature, silly and girlish. Beauty, Womens Product, Children Product, Fashion
6 Jacob Blue is the color of trust and peace. It can suggest loyalty and integrity as well as conservatism and frigidity. Accounting,Health Care, Technology, Security
7 Purple Purple is the color of the imagination. It can be creative and individual or immature and impractical. Religion, Psychic, Humanitarian
8 Brown The color brown is a friendly yet serious, down-to-earth color that relates to security, protection, comfort and material wealth. Agriculture, Construction, Legal
9 White White is color at its most complete and pure, the color of perfection. The color meaning of white is purity, innocence, wholeness and completion. convey a sense of purity, cleanliness, and peacefulness.
10 Gray gray is the color of compromise - being neither black nor white, it is the transition between two non-colors. It is unemotional and detached and can be indecisive. All Industries, Combination with other colors
11 Gray Black is the color of the hidden, the secretive and the unknown, creating an air of mystery. It keeps things bottled up inside, hidden from the world. All Industries, Combination with other colors
